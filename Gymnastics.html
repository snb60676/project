Gymnastics

Gymnastics is a sport that requires not only balance but also strength,flexibility,agility, endurance and coordination.

Gymnastics can be done competitively but also for fun! Involving four different events including; Vault, Bars, Beam, Floor. Competitive Gymnastics consist of 10 different levels. It is designed where each level adds harder skills and longer routines.

Levels 1-6 are considered compusary and invlove each gymnast doing the same routines. When you go to level 7 and up you are considered optional and can design and create your own routines but are required to have certain elements in each routine.
